ABOUT GRADIENT

Gradient is the brainchild of the first batch of Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning students of BMSCE.

The club aims to spread awareness among people about the importance of AI in various fields and cultivate interest in the latest technologies. This club also plans to conduct activities like workshops, guest lectures,hackathons,quizzes and many more.

The club has an exciting timeline mapped with conducting activities like workshops, guest lectures, hackathons, quizzes and many more. Gradient club members will be up-to-date with all new emerging technologies in the field of Artificial Intelligence.The club also undertakes efforts to increase literacy in the technologies of the future, by conducting introductory workshops in government schools.The club encourages implementation of AI technologies to solve real world problems and will be able to develop and implement prototype-level products based on the new technologies.
